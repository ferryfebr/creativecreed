JSCF 2025

JSCF 2025 Johor Smart City Forum 2025 was a strategic conference and networking event organized to support the development of smart, sustainable, and technology-driven cities in Johor. The forum brought together government agencies, industry leaders, technology providers, urban planners, investors, and academic institutions to discuss innovative solutions and future strategies for smart city transformation.

The event featured keynote sessions, panel discussions, exhibitions, networking opportunities, and technology showcases focusing on digital infrastructure, smart mobility, sustainable urban development, green technology, data-driven governance, cybersecurity, and intelligent city solutions. Johor Smart City Forum 2025 served as a collaborative platform to strengthen partnerships, encourage innovation, and accelerate Johor's vision towards becoming a connected, efficient, and sustainable smart state. ourservice-image/Visual Display.jpeg
